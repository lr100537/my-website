Minecraft


Minecraft is a 3D sandbox video game that has no required goals to accomplish, allowing players a large amount of freedom in choosing how to play the game.

Being made 2009 the game was very good for its time and there was no game like it.

The game after 15 whole years has gone far, from update 1.0 to 1.21



Fun fact: the game has a movie being made on it!

Minecraft music

The music from the game is very well known this includes: C418 by Aria Math, Pigstep by Lena Raine and one of the newer ones Creator also by Lena Raine.

Benefits of playing Minecraft

  • creativity is boosted
  • teamwork because of the multiplayer feature
  • engineering because you have to build shelter to survive
  • focus
