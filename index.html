What is Minecraft?

Minecraft is a 3D sandbox video game that has no required goals to accomplish, allowing players a large amount of freedom in choosing how to play the game.

When was it made?

Created in 2009, Minecraft was revolutionary and unique for its time.

Where is it now?

The game has evolved significantly, from update 1.0 to 1.21 over 15 years!
